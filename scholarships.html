Available Scholarships

1. Merit-Based Scholarships

These scholarships are awarded to students with outstanding academic achievements. No financial need is required.

  • Eligibility: Minimum GPA of 3.5 or equivalent.
  • Application Process:
    1. Submit your academic transcripts.
    2. Provide a personal statement outlining your achievements.
    3. Complete the online application form.
Apply Now

2. Need-Based Scholarships

These scholarships are aimed at students from low-income families who demonstrate financial need.

  • Eligibility: Proof of financial need and academic transcripts.
  • Application Process:
    1. Complete the financial aid form.
    2. Submit supporting documents such as tax records or income statements.
    3. Write a personal essay explaining your financial situation.
Apply Now

3. International Student Scholarships

Designed to support international students pursuing education in another country.

  • Eligibility: Must hold a valid student visa and meet the academic criteria.
  • Application Process:
    1. Submit proof of admission to an accredited institution.
    2. Provide a statement of purpose.
    3. Attach your passport copy and student visa.
Apply Now

4. Athletic Scholarships

For students who excel in sports and want to continue playing at a competitive level while pursuing higher education.

  • Eligibility: Demonstrated athletic ability and academic qualifications.
  • Application Process:
    1. Submit a video of your athletic performances.
    2. Provide references from coaches.
    3. Complete the scholarship application form.
Apply Now

5. Community Service Scholarships

These scholarships are awarded to students who have made a significant impact on their communities through volunteer work.

  • Eligibility: Evidence of community service activities and recommendations from community leaders.
  • Application Process:
    1. Submit a log of your community service hours.
    2. Provide two letters of recommendation.
    3. Write an essay about your community service experience.
Apply Now

6. STEM Scholarships

For students pursuing studies in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics fields.

  • Eligibility: Enrollment in an accredited STEM program.
  • Application Process:
    1. Submit proof of enrollment in a STEM program.
    2. Provide a statement of purpose outlining your career goals.
    3. Include letters of recommendation from professors or mentors.
Apply Now

7. Creative Arts Scholarships

For students with exceptional talent in visual arts, music, theater, or creative writing.

  • Eligibility: Portfolio of work or audition tape.
  • Application Process:
    1. Prepare a portfolio showcasing your work.
    2. Write a statement of purpose explaining your creative journey.
    3. Submit your application along with the portfolio.
