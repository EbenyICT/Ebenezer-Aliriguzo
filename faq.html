Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

General Questions

Q: What is Photo Hub?

A: Photo Hub is a platform for sharing and exploring photos.

Q: How do I upload a photo?

A: Navigate to the "Post Photo" page and upload your photo using the form provided.

Q: Is Photo Hub free to use?

A: Yes, Photo Hub offers free basic services. However, premium features may require a subscription.

Q: Can I delete a photo I uploaded?

A: Yes, you can delete any photo you uploaded by visiting your Profile page and managing your uploads.

Account Questions

Q: How do I reset my password?

A: Go to the Profile page and select "Change Password."

Q: How do I create an account?

A: Click on the "Signup" link in the navigation menu and fill in the required details.

Q: Can I deactivate my account?

A: Yes, you can deactivate your account by contacting our support team via the "Contact" page.

Technical Questions

Q: What image formats are supported?

A: Photo Hub supports popular formats like JPG, PNG, and GIF.

Q: Why can't I upload my photo?

A: Ensure your photo meets the size and format requirements. If the issue persists, contact support.

Q: How do I report a bug?

A: Navigate to the "Support" page and submit a detailed description of the issue you are facing.

Privacy and Security

Q: How is my data protected?

A: Photo Hub uses advanced encryption to protect your data and ensure your privacy.

Q: Can other users see my photos?

A: By default, uploaded photos are public. However, you can set specific photos to private in your Profile settings.

Q: What happens to my photos if I delete my account?

A: All your photos will be permanently deleted if you deactivate your account.

Subscription and Payments

Q: How do I subscribe to premium features?

A: Click on the "Subscribe" link in the navigation menu and choose your preferred plan.

Q: Can I cancel my subscription?

A: Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime from your Profile page under "Manage Subscription."

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other popular payment platforms.
